Features
1. Fitness Tracker: Heart Rate Monitoring, Blood Pressure and Oxygen Monitoring, Sleep Monitoring (Time and Quality). Details can be read in APP.

2. Multi sports mode including running, walking, step recording, calories burnt, distance tracking. Details can be read in APP.

3. Call reminder (Caller ID displays) /Information Reminder (Vibration Reminder) .

Push Notifications (including SMS, Facebook, Twitter, Skype, WhatsApp etc.)

4. Stopwatch ,Camera Remote Control, Bluetooth Music Control, Screen light adjustment, Sedentary Reminder, Multi-Watch Faces.

5. More functions include alarm reminder (three alarms could be set), sedentary reminder, flip
screen, do not disturb mode, anti-lost.

6. Clock, steps, heart rate, and calorie are displayed on the same page of screen for easy reading.

7. 1.3 inch Big HD IPS Full Touch Color Screen. Multi dials for selection.

8. Bluetooth 4.0(low power consumption).Support IOS 9.0 version above and Android 4.4 version and above.

9. Built-in 180mAh battery, working time is about 4~7 days. (The actual time depends on whether the screen is always on and how often the detection function is applied).

10. Life waterproof and dustproof (you can wear it when washing hands or in the rain even swimming. But not suitable for bathing and hot water or salt water)

11. Charging Method: Magnetic Attached Charging (USB Interface).Cable is delivered with the watch.

12. Wristband/Bracelet support language: English, French, German, Russian, Spanish, Japanese, Korean, Ukrainian (More languages to be added ).

13. APP Language Support Language: English, French, German, Italian, Russian, Spanish, Portuguese, Japanese, Korean, Ukrainian, Arabic.

APP to connect watch with phone (Heroband III)

The app for this watch is user friendly and compatible with both Android and Apple devices and is easy to connect the phone and the watch. Once it is connected via Bluetooth with the phone, the time and date will be automatically synchronized

Important Note: please ignore other Bluetooth devices on your phone before connecting the bracelet with the phone on the APP so that the watch can be found and connected. Remember to open the GPS/Location on your phone.
